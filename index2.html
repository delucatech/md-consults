Why Our Second Injury Fund Program?

  • MD Consults conducts yearly screenings of your staff for the Second Injury Fund and maintains the documentation.
  • The program’s medical team analyzes data and informs you of potential “at-risk” employees.
  • When an eligible employee is injured, we assist with the documentation required to qualify for state reimbursement.
